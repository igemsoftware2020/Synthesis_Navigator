Overview

Pathway Finder, a multifunctional platform for pathway construction, can assist synthetic biologists to find out the best routes in the ocean of metabolic reactions. Pathway Finder takes the Synthetic Bay as the data source and selects a heuristic algorithm——A* to make sure that this platform can provide the most accurate result in a short time. Thermodynamic feasibility, competition of heterogeneous reactions and toxicity of intermediate products have been taken as the important scoring items to optimize the pathways.

Users just need to input the start compound, target compound and weights of every index then Pathway Finder will search out the best several pathways and generate a report for you to download. Especially, Pathway Finder will provide the most suitable enzymes according to their physical and chemical properties including Tempreature, pH, Km and so on. Moreover, We add a new practical function ——reverse search to help find out all the compounds which can be transformed into the given target compound.
