We have built an incredible integrated database of Synthetic Biology for iGEMers & researchers.

Download Data
Overview

The Metabolic data in the Synthetic Bay is collected from five famous public Biology website——KEGG, BRENDA, eQuilibrator, MetaCyc and ChEBI. We pick the information that is most useful for synthetic biology to construct the database.

Synthetic Bay is composed of three tables, Enzyme, Compound and Reaction. Each table includes various properties which can satisfy users’ need for synthetic biology. The details of each table will be listed below and users can get this database by clicking the download links. Let’s swim into the ocean of data and come across the ones you’re interested in!!

Why choose Synthetic Bay?

More useful data and less redundancy

This year, our team expand the data source and add some new and important properties into the database. At the same time, we have removed the extremely incomplete data and fill in the missing data to reduce the search space.
